Crystal Collector

How To Play


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game. ("Number To Get")
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Sapphire
    • Emerald
    • Ruby
    • Citrine
    By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to "Your Total Score".
  3. You win the game by matching "Your Total Score" to "Number To Get".
  4. You lose the game if "Your Total Score" goes above "Number To Get".
  5. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  6. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
